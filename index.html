About Avalis

Welcome to Avalis! Avalis is a MMO RPG themed Dungeons and Dragons game.

Avalis is a land steeped in legend, where the echoes of ancient heroes and the whispers of arcane power intertwine with the fabric of reality. A world shaped by the ambitions of mortals and the will of the divine, Avalis is as vast as it is mysterious‚Äîa place where towering cities and sprawling wilds coexist with secrets waiting to be unearthed.

The heart of Avalis the world itself is completely encencapsulated in a large world tree like tower: a massive structure stretching far beyond mortal comprehension. Comprised of 100 distinct floors, each more dangerous and enigmatic than the last, the Tower stands as a challenge to adventurers and dreamers alike. It is said that the Tower holds treasures, knowledge, and power beyond measure, but it demands courage, skill, and unity to ascend its levels. Each floor is its own realm, shaped by themes both wondrous and perilous‚Äîmystical forests, fiery caverns, lost ruins, and even cities frozen in time. At the top of each floor waits a mighty Boss, a formidable guardian that must be defeated to unlock the way forward.

Your adventure begins at Valewatch Plains, the first floor of the Tower. It is a land of rolling hills, quaint villages, and a mysterious central mountain range concealing a mine brimming with resources‚Äîand danger. Here, you and your fellow adventurers will take your first steps toward greatness. The Ascendant Gate, an ancient magical gateway leading to the next floor, lies hidden within the heart of the Valewatch Plains, protected by The Hollow Warden, a foe of legend.

But the First Floor is only the beginning. Avalis is filled with towns and villages where trade thrives, libraries holding ancient knowledge, and ruins echoing with the tales of those who came before. As you climb the Tower, you will shape your story and uncover the mysteries of this world. Will you rise to be a hero, revered in song and story? Or will the Tower claim your name among the countless others who sought its heights and fell short?

Prepare to create your legacy. In Avalis, your choices matter, your courage will be tested, and your bonds with your allies will determine whether you conquer the Tower‚Äîor become part of its legend. The fate of Avalis awaits you, adventurer. Are you ready to ascend?
